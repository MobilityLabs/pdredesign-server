The PDredesign Data & Tech Analysis is a free tool for school districts to get the big picture of their data and technology systems that support professional learning. The Analysis completes the Data & Tech Landscape and brings together central office staff from across departments to discuss how current data systems, technology infrastructure, and product purchases documented in the Inventory are supporting teachers. The Analysis makes it easy to see how effectively technology is aligned to district needs and goals.

The Analysis may be used to:

  • Share knowledge and build consensus across departments
  • Gain a high-level insight into existing tools and capacities
  • Define technical requirements for new technology purchases
  • Understand return on investment for PD purchases
  • Surface challenges and successes in new technology implementation
  • Discover new products and resources recommended by EdSurge

About the Process

  1. Invite

    Automatically include participants from the Data & Tech Inventory and add new facilitators. You’ll be able to manage participants, send reminders, and schedule a meeting date.

  2. Analyze

    Discover how effectively existing products and data systems are supporting teachers. You or another facilitator will complete this during one or more in-person meetings with the team of participants you selected.

  3. Review Results

    After entering your team’s findings, you will immediately see your interactive, shareable results, where you may prioritize areas for impact, learn about supporting strategies and products, and add your reflections.
