The PDredesign Toolkit supports districts and schools to transform professional development systems so that they better support classroom teachers. The tools, including interactive assessments, surveys, and inventories, are co-designed with educators to meet their needs. The resulting reports provide users with actionable qualitative and quantitative data to design and implement a system to reach local goals. Combined reporting across tools helps districts connect the dots, and smart recommendations guide districts to take the next step.

Learn about the PDredesign tools

  1. Readiness Assessment

    The Readiness Assessment helps a district understand how its current PD system is structured and where to focus collective energy for improvement. Through a collaborative process Through a collaborative process, the Readiness Assessment brings together voices from the central office, school buildings, and classrooms to ensure transformation is grounded in a collective vision.

  2. Data & Tech Inventory

    The Data & Tech Inventory helps a district see how their data and technology systems support professional learning across departments. A small team of participants from different departments collaboratively puts together an inventory of the products and data systems in use.

  3. Data & Tech Analysis

    The Data & Tech Analysis takes the Inventory a step further by bringing together a small team to take a deep dive into how the data systems and technology support teacher professional development. The Analysis helps the team to collaboratively identify strengths and weaknesses in how their data systems and technology impact educators and then build a specific plan for the future. The Analysis connects users to EdSurge-recommended resources to support different areas of professional learning.
